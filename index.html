Who We Are

Locale wants you the shop local. We think you want to shop local because you know the benefites. If you don't we'll tell you a big one: local begets local. What do we mean? When you shop local you're not only helping out a neighbor, usually that money get used to pay for other locally sourced goods and services. Locale want to make it easier for you to do this by finding you the things you need based on whether that business is locally owned. Simply create a username and start asking our search engine to find you the things you need and where you need them. This will generate a list and tell you whether it's been claimed. If you choose to go to there remember to give yourself a point and we'll even set up an account for you to track where you've been. Have fun!
